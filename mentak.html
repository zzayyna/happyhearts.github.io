MENTAL WELLNESS

 

There is a lot that goes into your mental health and when it is properly taken care of, it can help you cope with stress, work productively, make better choices, and live better overall lives. Mental health is not a condition that just develops overnight ,your mental health affects how you think, feel, and act – and it can impact the people around you. With that in mind (no pun intended), let’s learn to become aware of our mental health and how we can practice improving it. Below are some things we would love for you to try. Enjoy!

 



❤️ SELF REFLECTION❤️

Self-reflection is an essential skill for personal growth. Without it, we walk around unconscious and often reactive to others and even our own selves. If you have ever had an emotional response to something or blurted out words that you later regretted, you can see how self-reflection might assist you in choosing more healthy responses and changing behaviors (even thoughts) that aren’t working well for you! If we take the time to better understand ourselves and who we are, we may find ourselves living happier and more satisfied!


TIP

Don't be afraid to try new things!

ACTIVITY #1

Journaling – it’s a great way to learn more about yourself!

ACTIVITY #2

Take some personality quizzes!







❤️ RELAXATION ❤️

Relaxation is an important part of maintaining health and well-being and being able to calmly deal with life’s stresses. It improves your mental health and gives the body a chance to take a break, releasing muscle tension (that you may not even be aware of), and lowering blood pressure. Massage, exercise, meditation, reading books, craftwork, guided imagery, and yoga are all methods of achieving relaxation. Choose something that you enjoy, that gives you a sense of satisfaction, and that you feel best relaxes both your body and mind.


TIP

Incorporate breaks into your daily routine!

ACTIVITY #1

Listen to some music, or entertain yourself with some reading, or a movie!

ACTIVITY #2

Incorporate meditation into your daily schedule - it's a great way to relieve stress!




ACTIVITY #3

This may sound childish, but it's so much more helpful than you think - try coloring!







❤️ SOCIALIZATION ❤️

Socialization is the process of learning how to become part of a society. Through socialization, one learns the society’s unwritten codes of conduct, their role in life, and what is expected from them. Socialization occurs when one interacts with other people, and it is a very important process in the forming of personality - without socialization we have no commonly recognizable sense of self. Having a balance of alone time and social time can have profound impacts on our mental health!


TIP #1

Take advantage of weekends and holidays to spend time with your family and friends!

TIP #2

Don’t forget to smile at the people around you (even if you don’t know them). Your smile alone could make someone's day!

ACTIVITY

Text your friends right now, maybe even make plans to hang out!







❤️ PRODUCTIVITY ❤️

The importance of productivity is down to how much it impacts your personal and professional life. Time management is organizing your day so that you find the best use for every moment. Excellent time management allows you to create a healthy balance in your workflow and home life. The consequences of failed time management include missing deadlines and living with excessive stress.


TIP #1

Make lists to organize your thoughts and plans (you can even use these checklists we provide!)

TIP #2

Routines have been proven to significantly reduce stress and increase productivitiy levels for individuals. Use our habit tracker to make sure you are consistent in your healthy habits!

TIP #3

Planning ahead is key! You can use our weekly planner to plan your week ahead of time. This way, you have a clear idea of what you need to accomplish as you start each day!







❤️ SELF-ESTEEM ❤️

Our self esteem is our confidence in ourselves and our abilities. Being confident in ourselves enables us to establish a healthy relationship with ourselves, and our relationship with ourselves affects our interactions with others. Having healthy self-esteem makes us better able to deal with life’s ups and downs, and significantly improves our overall well-being.


TIP #1

Talk to someone — it could be family, a friend, or even a therapist.

TIP #2

Practice positive self-talk daily, preferably in front of your mirror as you get ready — this works wonders in boosting your confidence!

ACTIVITY

Write a list of things you love about yourself!
