❤️ WHO ARE WE? ❤️


We are three Kode with Klossy scholars, who want to make a difference in this world by reminding people to take time for themselves! We can empathize with anyone who ever feels overwhelmed, and believe that it's really important to take a break and find time to take care of yourself.



❤️ OUR GOAL ❤️


Our goal is to provide users with resources through which they can be motivated, inspired, and comforted even when they feel that their life is in chaos. We want to remind you that you are not alone in your struggles, and that it is possible to take control over your life again!




❤️ OUR PLAN ❤️


Our website has information, tips, interactivity, and other resources that can help you take the first steps toward a healthier lifestyle. Additionally, we invite you to contribute to the cause by spreading the word or donating! We hope that this website will, over the years, become a trustworthy go-to resource for anyone feeling stressed or unmotivated. Taking care of yourself, both mentally and physically, matters!
